CORRUGATED BOXES

Innova has specialized in the field of micro and B Flute, by providing the possibility for offset printed high quality paper boards to be laminated to corrugated sheets. Our team of highly skilled professionals are well versed with suggesting suitable options for a varied range of products like automobile parts, Phone boxes, toys, or any other material which need robust packaging at low cost without any compromise in the printing quality. Corrugated is made up of three layers of paper that includes an inside liner, an outside liner, and fluting with a ruffled shape, which runs in between the two. It is made up of 3 ply / 5ply / 7 ply. Higher the ply, greater the strength of the box. It is a sturdy packaging material made of three layers of paper. During manufacturing, the inner sheet is put through the corrugation process to create 'S' shaped flutes depending on the thickness; and attached to a VK/SK/WKL liner base forming a 2 ply corrugated sheet. Then a printed sheet is pasted on the flute side to form a 3ply box, similar process is followed for 5 ply and 7 ply box. The flutes used in manufacturing these boxes play a vital role. Different types of flutes are used in making corrugated boxes. However, Flute C is the most important one which is widely used in making printed fluted cartons. Using different types of flutes help you serve the various purposes.
